Academic

Our curriculum and academic programs

Academic

Academic Excellence

The school provides curriculum from nursery to 10th grades and more than 30 activities have been conducted by the school which helps in the overall development of the students.

Latest teaching techniques and tools like Educomp Smart Classes, Abacus Classes, Graphic Animation, Simulations, 3D Images, Video Clip in addition to traditional chalk and talk methods of teaching are used by the teachers.

Co-curricular

Various Co-curricular activities like Quiz, Competition, Debates, Singing and Dancing classes etc. School also runs hobby classes for the students like Sports, Art Aid Crafts, Music, Dance hours etc.

Events & Competitions

School celebrates all national festivals and few religious festivals every year. In the last of the session school celebrates the achievements and organizes a grand annual function for the fun and joy of the students, teachers and parents.

Every year school participates in various inter school competitions. School also conducts innumerable competitions like Quiz, Debate, Writing, Drawing Competition, Rakhi making and Rangoli making etc.

Examinations

Examinations

  • (i) The Scheme of Examinations and Pass Criteria for All India/Delhi Senior School Certificate and All India/Delhi Secondary Examinations conducted by the Board, shall be as laid down from time to time.
  • (ii) Class XI/IX examinations shall be conducted internally by the schools themselves.
  • (iii) The Board will conduct the external examinations at the end of Class XII/Class X.
  • (iv) Class XII/Class X examinations will be based on the syllabi as prescribed by the Board for class XI/IX respectively from time to time.
  • (v) Number of papers, duration of examination and marks for each subject/paper will be as specified in the curriculum for the year.
  • (vi) The Examination would be conducted in theory as well as in practicals, depending upon the nature of the subject(s) and the marks/grades allotted shall be as prescribed in the curriculum.
  • (vii) Marks/grades shall be awarded for individual subjects and the aggregate marks shall not be given.
