Gyandeep Public School is a private co-educational English medium school located at Dharsauna Cholapur Varanasi in sprawling 2 acres land.

It is run by Mr. Manoj Kumar Jaiswal. The school was started in 2003 and was first English medium school in the area. School offers various facilities and opportunities for the students to explore their latest talents. The school offers curriculum from nursery to 10th grades. The strength of the school is 1500 supported teachers.

We believe in quality education and we provide a friendly and education learning environment to the students.
