About Our School

We Are Awesome Team

Gyandeep Public School is a private co-educational English medium school located at Dharsauna Cholapur Varanasi in sprawling 2 acres land. It is run by Mr. Manoj Kumar Jaiswal. The school was started in 2003 and was first English medium school in the area.

School offers various facilities and opportunities for the students to explore their latest talents. The school offers curriculum from nursery to 10th grades. The strength of the school is 1500 supported by committed and qualified teachers.

Big green campus with spacious ventilated classrooms
Day boarding classes for weak students
Smart Class & Abacus class with modern technology
Hobby classes — Dance, Music, Craft, Sports
Science Lab & Computer Lab with transport facility

The Mission: Aims & Objectives

Over a century and decade of education legacy was bequeathed to the holy city of Varanasi when Gyandeep English School announced the starting of its session in 1999. The torch of learning has been passed from generation and we have grown qualitatively and numerically.

Gyandeep combines the educational dimensions with the firm mooring of evocative nationalism and culturalism. It is designed to impart integrated education to children, drawing the best from Indian culture and tradition.
