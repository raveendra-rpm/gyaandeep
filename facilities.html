Our Facilities

School provides all the facilities with respect to students which affect health, behaviour, engagement, learning and growth in achievement.

Special Educational Classes

To enhance the way of learning, school provides 3 special classes along with Academics:

  • Educomp Smart Class
  • Abacus Classes
  • Spoken Classes

School Garden

The entire school is flanked with lovely garden that gives the school a pleasant atmosphere and plenty of space to students to play during break times.

Big Ventilated Classrooms

School provides big and ventilated classrooms equipped with CCTV cameras with proper seating arrangements.

Library

School has library containing learning and teaching resources that are available for all students.

Laboratories

The school has three laboratories well equipped for enhancing practical applications, demonstrations and experimentations:

  • Science Lab
  • Maths Lab
  • Computer Lab

Music & Dance

Two rooms to support music & singing are there with recorders, speakers and traditional musical instruments.

Sports

School provides various indoor and outdoor games for students like Ludo, Carrom, Chess, Badminton, Football, Volleyball, Basketball, Skates etc.

Medical Care

All cases of illness or injury / first aid is there with medical attention. Rest rooms and emergency transport is available to take students to hospital.

Over All Development

School holds more than 30 activities to develop writing skills, speaking skills, reading skills and many more. INTACH provides awareness among students to save their culture and traditional values.

Smart School Systems

School has two smart systems:

  • Smart Card Based Attendance System
  • Gyandeep School Android App

Transport

Transport facility is available with GPS tracking for safe and reliable commute of students.

Water, Hygiene & Sanitation

Adequate level of clean and fresh water supply and full hygiene and sanitation is maintained for the students with respect to Health & Hygiene Programme.
