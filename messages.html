Director's Message

Director Desk

It is my pleasure to welcome you to the GPS. This is a school where we are deeply committed to ensuring that every day each and every student has the attention and care of our professional staff. We work to foster a school environment that is supportive and encouraging for both children and adults.

At GPS, we want to provide an exemplary educational experience that nurtures a perspective developed through the challenging program. Not only do our students demonstrate excellent academic progress and achievement while at GPS, they become grounded in the important qualities of integrity, leadership, adaptability, resilience, and true compassion for others. Additionally, our students are active caretakers of the environment.

The unique and beautiful campus is the backdrop for a school determined to have a profoundly positive impact on the personal lives of its students as well as assuring a great education. We are focused on being a student and learning centered school that is consistently moving forward in preparing students to be successful as modern global citizens.

God bless!
Profound Regards
Manoj Kumar Jaiswal
