T.C. Information

Transfer Certificate

Parents/Guardians can apply for a Transfer Certificate for their ward by following the process below:

1

Submit Application

Submit a written application to the school office addressed to the Principal requesting T.C. for your ward.

2

Clear Dues

Ensure all pending fees and dues are cleared before the T.C. can be issued.

3

Return School Property

Return all school property including library books, ID card, and any other materials.

4

Collect T.C.

The Transfer Certificate will be issued within 7 working days from the date of application.

Note: For any queries related to Transfer Certificate, please contact the school office at 0543-2612688 or 9838173720.
